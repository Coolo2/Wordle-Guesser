How to use (Mobile)
  • Tap on the box to type the letters. Copy them from your wordle game.

  • Continue until the answer is correct, then click the tick to reset
How to use (PC)
  • Click on the box to type the letters. Copy them from your wordle game.
  • Right click, or use Ctrl, Option, Alt or Space to update color
  • Use one of the answers provided on the right on the next row
  • Continue until the answer is correct, then click the tick to reset
